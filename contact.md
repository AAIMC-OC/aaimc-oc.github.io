## Collaborate with us

AAIMC‑OC is a global research platform bringing together clinicians, researchers, and data scientists to advance equitable AI‑driven diagnostics in ovarian cancer.

We welcome collaboration with:

- Clinical teams interested in contributing data or participating in studies  
- Researchers working in AI, pathology, genomics, or global health  
- Institutions looking to adopt federated learning approaches  
- Organisations focused on improving equitable access to cancer diagnostics  

<br>

## Expand the network

We are actively seeking partners to expand the use of federated learning for cancer research across diverse healthcare systems.

In particular, we are interested in:

- Multi‑site data collaborations using interoperable data standards  
- Evaluation of AI tools across different populations and imaging systems  
- Implementation of low‑cost and scalable diagnostic approaches  
- Development of governance frameworks for international data collaboration  

<br>

## Join AAIMC‑OC

If you are interested in joining the collaboration, please contact us with a brief description of your:

- Institution and area of expertise  
- Proposed area of collaboration  
- Relevant datasets, infrastructure, or research interests  

We aim to build a **collaborative, inclusive, and globally distributed research network**, and welcome partnerships from both high‑income and low‑ and middle‑income settings.
